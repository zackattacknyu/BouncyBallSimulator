















In the first screen, the bar tells you how fast the ball
	will move when launched. Press Enter to set the speed here.
In the next screen, you launch the ball. 
First, use the left, right, up and down arrow keys
	to determine the launching point and
	press enter when you have decided.
The pink rectangle represents the area where you 
	can launch the ball from.
Then, you decide the angle it will be launched at.
	The arrow points in the direction that it 
	will be launched at. Press 'w' to rotate the
	arrow counter-clockwise and 's' to rotate the
	arrow clockwise.
While the ball is moving, press any key to exit.  
Press Enter to Continue.
Press 'e' or 'E' to exit.
